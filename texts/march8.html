Nocturne & Shadowheart

Shadowheart and Nocturne were at the conclave. Both of them were friends since they saw each other. The other sharrans apprentice always teased Shadowheart for her name choice. They always bullied her for that. Today, was one of the worst days of that bullying. She got beaten down by a few older girls. She tried to stand tall. They said to her. “Do you actually think, chosing that name will make the Lady Shar love you more. You’re pathetic” Said one of the leaders before striking her in her leg and Shadowheart felt on the ground. It was a great name, she was proud to have chosen that one, even tho the others might not like it. She saw Nocturne, that little purple tiefling held out a hand to her. “Thank you” She looked at them and said. “What are friends for?” She was so happy to have Nocturne as a friend.
Nocturne led Shadowheart somewhere she’s never been. “I want to show you something.” She was watching as there was that little hideout full of night orchids. It was so beautiful. Nocturne, placed a little blanket on the grass. “It’s beautiful” Nocturne smiled at the half elf. “I found that place a few days ago. I wanted to show it to you.” This was something that meant a lot for Shadowheart. Nocturne meant a lot to her. She touched gently the tiefling’s hand. “Thank you for everything” Shadowheart hugged Nocturne. The tiefling pulled out two books and there was a huge smile on the half elf’s face. “You know me so well.”
This was the only memory Shadowheart had from her childhood, and she was so happy to be able to share this with her friends, the people who grew closer among their adventure. She leaned on Wyll’s shoulder when she told this story.
