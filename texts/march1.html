Astarion's parents

Astarion grew up in a wealthy family. His family always looked out that he had everything he needed except what he may have needed the most: affection. He grew up sheltered from the world beyond the gates of the upper city. His mother and his father were always working and making sure that their son lived up to their expectation.
Finding his own way was something that was quite complicated for the high elf. His father was himself a magistrate and he wanted his son to follow in his footpath. Even tho Astarion had no real idea how the real world worked beyond the gates of the upper city he became a magistrate with an education but not enough knowledge on the world to make good decisions.
In general, his parents were always more focused on keeping their public image and spoiling their son with gifts and money rather than actually focusing on him. They may not even have realized that one day he just disapeared after Cazador enslaved him.
