One of the companions has been gone from camp for a very long time

It's been a few hours, since Astarion said he would be back soon for trying to find something on four legs to feed. Wyll started to get worried about the elf. He was in his tent, he cared deeply about Astarion, but he didn't know how he could tell him this. Wyll got up and started to change shoes to go look for the spawn. There were so many thoughts in his head that something could have gone wrong, what if he went after something too big for him. Wyll knew that Astarion was more than capable of defending himself in front of an animal, but that didn't stop his mind from painting tragedies. He closed his eyes and started to walk toward the forest, the place where he last saw Astarion before he ventured into the forest to feed himself.
After sometimes, he managed to find the elf and he was fine. He let out a deep sight of relief. Astarion was leaning against a rock, it seemed he was able to find something to eat. “Are you alright, Astarion?” Asked the human. It hasn’t been very long since he first met the elf, but he couldn’t help it to try to make sure everyone was alright. Astarion met Wyll’s gaze and the human saw something in his eyes, it wasn’t hunger, it was sadness and anxiety. “Why are you still up Wyll?” He knelt in front of Astarion. “I was worried about you.” There was no point in lying about why he was here. Wyll asked, “Is it okay if I put my hand on your shoulder?” They had talked about what happened to Astarion in his past, and he wanted to make sure he would be comfortable with physical touch. “I…” This question surprised Astarion because he was so used to people, well, mostly Cazador using him to get what he wanted. Never asking, it was something new for the elf, but he welcomed the question and also the gesture. “Yes” When Astarion gave Wyll the answer, he gently put his hand on Astarion’s shoulder. It was a comforting touch. “If you ever want to talk about anything. You know that I’m here”
Astarion never had someone look out for him like Wyll is doing right now. It was such a new sensation, to be cared for, to be loved. Old instinct were trying to kick in and just tried to flirt and seduce him. He cared for the human more than what he would like to admit. Just Wyll touching so delicately, so carefully his shoulder. This made him smile. “Thank you Wyll” Wyll smiled and simply said “What else are friends for?” Right, they were friends and nothing more. He saw Wyll get up and offer him a hand to help him up, and he happily took this hand. The moon was almost inviting them to spend more time here, together with this silvery glow making the night almost magical. They got back to camp and each went back to their own tent separately. “Good night Astarion” Astarion saw Wyll’s smile, and he smiled back at him. “Sleep well, Wyll”
Wyll was beating himself for saying the word friends once he got back into his own tent. It was true that they were friends, but he did want more. He knew what he could do, prepare something that maybe Astarion would like…
