Karlach gets angry, why?

Karlach looked with horror at what just happened in front of her. She couldn’t believe what they just did. They really just condemned Wyll to be a lemur fighting for Zariel in the blood war. She turned her eyes to Tav. “With just a press of a leaver. You killed Wyll.” Karlach was furious about what just happened. She actually had a hard time believing that Tav would do that. Karlach adored Wyll, that man gave up so much for her to keep her alive. She was filed with a mix of different emotions, sadness, grief and anger. When Tav tried to approach her after this, she just backed away. “No, give me time. A lot of it.” She sounded cold and angry at the same time.
In her mind, she had a really hard time forgiving what they just did. Astarion was right next to her when this happened, and he laid a hand on her shoulder and just whispered. “I’m sorry, he deserved better.” She could see the hurt in his eyes. Karlach knew how much Wyll meant to Astarion. These two were growing closer by the minute. Karlach was furious at Tav for doing this. They had no right to do this to Wyll, even if that meant letting Mizora live. For the rest of the colony, she didn’t talk much, wasn’t very enthusiastic about anything. When they faced Ketheric Thorm, she saw him : Gortash. What was that fucker doing here? Was he somehow behind this? She couldn’t believe her eyes. That man sold her to Zariel 10 years ago. She was enraged. How he spoke, she picked up that he followed Bane, the god of tyranny. She backed away, realising she had been protecting a banite before he sold her to Zariel. All she thought was that she was so blind to have not seen it earlier. She couldn’t wait to flatten him and get her revenge.
